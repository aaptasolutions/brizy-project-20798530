🧸 SAFETY IS PRIMARY

Secure entry systems, fencing around play areas, and childproofing of facilities to prevent accidents.

CCTV surveillance to enhance safety and security.

Well-referenced, trained and qualified staff members, with police verification completed.

Active supervision to prevent accidents and ensure children are always within sight. Staff-to-child ratios are maintained to ensure adequate supervision.

Easily accessible fire safety protocols.

Regular handwashing, proper sanitation of toys and equipment, and policies for handling sick children to prevent the spread of contagious illnesses.

Limited access for unauthorised persons.

Open communication with parents is essential for ensuring the safety of preschool students. Preschools provide regular updates on safety procedures, incidents, and any changes in policies or protocols.
