Why Care Bears Academy?

Welcome to Care Bears Academy, where absolute love for children is at the heart of everything we do!

At Care Bears Academy, we learn through play because we believe play is the natural work of young children, and a play-based curriculum effortlessly fosters creativity, curiosity, and a love for learning. We do not encourage rote learning. We want our students to love school as much as we enjoy having them with us. Our approach is not just about teaching children; it's about creating an environment where they learn to see the world through eyes of wonder and also receive the skills to thrive emotionally, socially, and intellectually.

We are aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 guidelines, ensuring that our teaching methods are modern, developmentally appropriate, inclusive, and geared towards holistic development. With a strong emphasis on early literacy and reading skills, we empower children to explore the magical world of books, igniting their imagination and laying a strong foundation for lifelong reading habits.

We believe children are precious, and each child is a gift waiting to be opened and cherished by loving and trustable adults. Through personalised education plans and tailored learning experiences, we celebrate diversity and ensure that every child feels seen, heard, and absolutely valued. Our goal is not just to educate, but to instill a lifelong love for self, life, and learning in every child that walks through our doors.

At Care Bears Academy, we are intentional about training our children on essential life skills such as empathy, communication, and cooperation. We use group activities, shared meaningful interactions, to help our children to follow the pathways of teamwork, healthy friendships, and respect for others inevitably learning to live in community.

We are also committed to community building. We believe in partnering with families to create a supportive social network

SOCIAL & EMOTIONAL LEARNING

Emotional health heavily correlates with academic success. Children who feel good emotionally are simply better able to pay attention, control themselves, and get along with others, which can improve how well they do in school. Good emotional health in childhood also enables better lifelong mental and emotional well-being, reducing later mental health problems such as anxiety, depression, and other dangerous behaviors. Well-adjusted kids possess a blend of self-awareness, resilience, empathy, and confidence to help them manage life's hard knocks in a composed and mature manner.

The emotional health of our childhood determines our adult emotional well-being, as well as the success of our relationships, our mental and physical health, and our professional and family dynamics. Emotional health as a child is a very important factor in putting people on the path for a fulfilling and meaningful life in adulthood.
