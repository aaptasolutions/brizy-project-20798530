TUITIONS @ LEARNERS HUB @ KOTHANUR

Learners HUB is a center for student education. We are focused on augmenting the mainstream school education.  We provide par excellence coaching for children from standard / grade 1st to 10th. We at Learners HUB are focused on giving all the support your child needs to see that he / she excelsin their subjects. We provide learning support for students for all subjects in their curriculum. We can also provide support for those subjects where the ward has difficulty. Learners HUB is a haven for students who want to learn in a fun way.

Teachers @ Learners HUB

We are focused on developing a pool of teachers who are passionate about their subjects and also who love working with children to see that the child excels in his / her studies. All our teachers are experienced and qualified to augment the learning experience. We aim to simplify concepts so that the child understands the logic. We also work towards training a child to be fully equipped to face the exam paper confidently. Our team consists of B.Ed. qualified teachers, engineers, doctors, and postgraduates in various fields who are passionate about teaching their subjects. Our teachers understand the issues of exam pressure and they also will address the challenges associated with writing and facing exams.

Tuitions ONLINE @ Learners HUB

We provide online tuition services across the globe for all types of syllabuses including State, ICSE, CBSE, and IGCSE boards. We use the best of technologies to make the learning experience smooth and clear. The advantages of classes online includes time saved on travel, access to the best teachers, and of course the GREEN planet cause. In a busy city like Bangalore, losing an hour in traffic is a real loss of precious time for the parent and the child. Therefore, we felt it necessary for us to provide this platform so that we can provide quality education to our children.

Private & Group Tuitions

Leaders HUB provides both personal one-on-one tuitions and group coaching as well. Our groups go to a maximum of 5 students in a batch. We believe both have its advantages. The former is useful for those students who have difficulty with a specific subject and need slow learning pathway and more focus on understanding concepts. The group has the advantage of community learning, where the child learns as the other children learn. In every setting, we make it imperative that every member of the group has understood the concepts. We specialize in one-to-one tuitions. These are focused on children who need that extra attention to make it through the year. The private tutors deliver high quality education online. One to one is more expensive than the group coaching.

Special Teaching Services Catching up after COVID

We at Leaders HUB are also at the service of those parents who have children who have suffered severe setback by COVID years where the education was severely compromised and your child lost the prime time of learning fundamental concepts and are academically compromised and challenged. We would love to take on the challenge and bring those children up to speed so that they can enjoy a normal educational journey for the rest of their learning lives. During COVID most schools moved to online teaching and most teachers were not equipped or trained to conduct online classes and children were not used to being taught online. This created a huge gap in the conceptual understanding of the curriculum and most children who were in the formative years like preschool and grade 1 have been severely affected and are now struggling to grasp the curriculum at higher grades. Also, there are children who had health situations because of which they lost out on those key years. These children need special attention. We are specialized at getting such children fast tracked to cover up their bases and catch up with their classes and move on confidently.

Open School @ Leaders HUB

Learners HUB is also a school for those who parents who would like to opt for an alternative schooling for their children. We provide open schooling options where the student can privately write the exam and we can provide the teaching part in collaboration with parents. This would be an excellent option for those who are homeschooling parents. We believe that the parents should play a key role in the education of their children but we know that all parents may not have the skill sets to teach their children all the subject, and that is where Leaders HUB can help. We will provide all the support for those parents who would like to try this alternative path. Regular schooling has classes that are packed with students and there is little scope for teachers to provide adequate attention for the children. Also, the spirit of unhealthy competition that is fostered in the schools of today and the pressure that is on the children to perform is unfortunate and harmful to the spirit of a child. It is our strong belief that a child needs to compete only with his / her own past performance. All students have not started at the same starting point and therefore it would be unjust to give a common finishing line. The entire world is in a rat race and we are teaching our children to enter and survive in that rat race in our schools. Instead of focusing on helping children excel at understanding concepts in a manner that matches with their intelligence types, we push and stuff logical learning down their throats, and appreciate only those who make it through. Our education systems only evaluate the mathematical and logical intelligence / capacity of the child, whereas it has been cleared proved that the child may be good in more than one of the other intelligences, for example visual-spatial, linguistic-verbal, body-kinesthetic, musical, interpersonal, intrapersonal, and naturalistic.

Leaders HUB is here to provide that alternate avenue to do schooling in a way that would be enjoyable, learning concepts in a way that children grow to apply and discover what their real calling in life is. We strongly believe that we can rear a generation of children who would love the world and explore it not with an intent to win but to serve with passion and a love arising from awe of the magnificently complex world around them.
